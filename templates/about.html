Our Story

Hoy Sports Data, founded by Brayden Hoy in 2025, aims to provide affordable, high-speed, and easy-to-use analytics applications to every level of football. Our unique, in-game software helps to provide professional level data in seconds through easy input capable of being operated by any staff member with minimal training. Hoy, a former NCAA athlete and analyst, designs the software with low-staffed, low-budget teams in mind. HSD believes in personal relationships and communication, which is why we provide client representatives to each school using our software to help you execute, understand, and troubleshoot your software usage in order for your team to be more successful on gameday.
